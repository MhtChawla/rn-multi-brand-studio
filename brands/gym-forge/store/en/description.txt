Forge Athletic rewards you for every purchase you make. Points add up automatically, and your membership tier rises as you spend — giving you access to better rewards the longer you stay active.

Track every transaction and point balance in your activity history. No guesswork, no missing credits. Every purchase is logged and accounted for.

When you're ready to redeem, browse available rewards and apply your points at checkout. The process is straightforward: choose a reward, confirm, done.

Your digital membership card lives in the app. Pull up the QR code at any Forge Athletic location and the cashier scans it in seconds. No physical card to carry, no account number to memorize.

Tier progression gives you a clear target. See exactly how many points stand between you and the next level, and what changes when you get there. Higher tiers mean better redemption rates and priority access to new products.

Forge Athletic is built for people who train consistently and want their spending to reflect that commitment. Every rep counts. So does every dollar.

Features:
- Earn points automatically on every purchase
- Progress through membership tiers based on cumulative spend
- View full activity history with transaction-level detail
- Redeem points for rewards directly in the app
- Digital membership card with scannable QR code
