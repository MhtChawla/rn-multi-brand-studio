Café Aurora Rewards tracks every purchase you make and turns it into something worth coming back for.

Earn points each time you buy. Watch your balance grow with every coffee, pastry, and lunch order. When you have enough, redeem them for free drinks, food, and more — directly through the app.

Your membership tier reflects how often you visit. As you spend more, you move into higher tiers that carry greater value per purchase. The app shows you exactly where you stand and what it takes to reach the next level.

Every transaction appears in your activity history. You can review past purchases, point earnings, and redemptions at any time. Nothing disappears, and nothing requires you to keep a paper receipt.

Your digital membership card lives in the app. Open it at the counter, let the barista scan the QR code, and your points are added automatically. No physical card to carry, no account number to recite.

Café Aurora Rewards is built for people who visit regularly and want to get more from each visit. It stays out of your way when you do not need it and gives you clear, accurate information when you do.
